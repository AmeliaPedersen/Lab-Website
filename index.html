Bug and Insect

Many people are confused about what is an insect and what is a bug.As reconnectwithnature.org says "While you may use the words bug and insect interchangeably, it's not really accurate, because while all bug are insects not all insects are bugs"

So what is an insect then? An insect is a group of arthropods which have 3 main body parts: A head, a thorax and an abdomen.Other arthropoda classes that are not insects include arachnida (spider), crustacea, and myriopada (millipedes and centipedes) So spiders and centipedes are not insects.

So what is a bug? "an insect of a large order distinguished by having mouthparts that are modified for piercing and sucking" according to Oxford dictionary

Spiders are from another arthropod class called arachnida.
